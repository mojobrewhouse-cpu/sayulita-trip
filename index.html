💡 Good to Know

💵 Bring Mexican pesos — many restaurants don't take cards. ATMs are available in town. US dollars accepted but at bad rates.
🚐 Airport shuttle is ~1 hour. Pre-book for $50-89 USD, or grab the COMPOSTELA bus for $3-4 (white/green bus, "Sayulita" on windshield, runs every 20 min).
🚗 Skip the rental car — Sayulita is a walking town. Narrow streets, no parking. Everything is walkable.
🌞 Pack reef-safe sunscreen (required for Marietas Islands), a hat, and comfortable walking sandals.
📱 Check your cell plan for Mexico coverage. Most US carriers include Mexico, but double-check roaming.
🍽️ Main dishes average ~250 pesos ($13 USD) at mid-range spots. Street food is even cheaper and delicious.
